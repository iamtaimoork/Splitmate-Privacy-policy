We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Types of Data Collected

This Usage Data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), device type, operating system version, the features of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those features, and other diagnostic data. This data is collected to improve application stability and performance.
